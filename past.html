About Me



Hi, I’m Zijun James Chen, a Computer Science (Honours) student at the University of Technology Sydney, specialising in Artificial Intelligence and Data Analytics. I’m passionate about building intelligent systems, solving real-world problems with code, and continuously improving my technical skills across multiple domains.

I have hands-on experience in deep learning, C++/Python development, and game engines, with projects ranging from a 3D ResNet-based lip-reading model to full game recreations and interactive systems in Unity and Roblox Studio. Game development has always been a huge part of what drives me. There’s something uniquely rewarding about bringing ideas to life, making systems interactive and fun, and crafting experiences that people can interact with and enjoy.

At my core, I’m motivated by learning through building. I love taking ambitious ideas, breaking them down, and iterating until they become polished, working systems. Whether I’m optimising a CNN, designing game logic, or refining a user experience, I take pride in every attempt I make, whether it is failures that push me to grow and successes that fuel my drive to keep improving.

I’m now seeking an internship where I can contribute to a team, grow as an engineer, and apply my skills to real-world software challenges. I bring strong problem-solving skills, a growth mindset, and genuine enthusiasm for creating things that work well, look good, and feel great to use.
